How it works

Four signals. One score.

Every creative gets analysed across four independent AI pipelines in parallel — then synthesised into a single, actionable report.

01
Neural attention
DeepGaze IIE
Predicts a heatmap of where viewers will look, trained on MIT/CAT2000 eye-tracking datasets with millions of real fixations.
02
Scanpath sequence
Peak detection + inhibition
Predicts viewing order — face first? headline? CTA? — so you know what viewers see in the first second of exposure.
03
Semantic understanding
Claude 4.6 vision
Identifies brand, audience, offer, tone, and competitors — and reveals whitespace gaps competitors haven't exploited.
04
CARF scoring
Research-backed framework
Combines signals into 5 dimensions: Attention, Emotion, Cognitive Load, Memory, Decision — graded against 200+ research benchmarks.
What you see

Every creative gets an executive summary in seconds.

Upload any ad — image or video. Get back a scanpath prediction, brand analysis, audience insight, and the single most promising differentiation angle.

Pipeline 02
Scanpath prediction
We predict the sequence of fixations — what your viewer looks at first, second, third, fourth. The first second of exposure is where most ads win or lose attention.
Pipeline 01
Attention heatmap
A probability map of where viewers' eyes land across the creative — produced by DeepGaze IIE, a neural model trained on millions of real eye-tracking fixations. Hot zones get seen. Cold zones don't.
