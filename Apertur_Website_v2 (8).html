How it works

Four signals. One score.

Every creative gets analysed across four independent AI pipelines in parallel — then synthesised into a single, actionable report.

01
Attention heatmap
Neural eye-tracking
Predicts a heatmap of where viewers will look, trained on millions of real eye-tracking fixations.
02
Scanpath sequence
Peak detection + inhibition
Predicts viewing order — face first? headline? CTA? — so you know what viewers see in the first second of exposure.
03
Semantic understanding
AI creative read
Identifies brand, audience, offer, tone, and competitors — and reveals whitespace gaps competitors haven't exploited.
04
Creative verdict
Research-backed framework
Combines signals into 5 dimensions: Attention, Emotion, Cognitive Load, Memory, Decision — graded against 200+ research benchmarks.
